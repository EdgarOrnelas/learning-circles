How will this all go down?

Every week you’ll join your study group for a meeting of minds (and regular high fives) in your local library branch. You’ll be guided through the online materials by a community organiser, who will help facilitate collaboration with your fellow learners.

How long will it take?

The courses will run for between 5-7 weeks. However, the pace is largely up to you. You can always slow down or speed up - the materials are there to be used and you can use them however you want.

About

We believe that people learn better when they’re learning together, and we’ve designed the study groups to provide the maximum support from the library and project staff as you work your way through some of the best free online educational content on the Web. As you move through the course, you’ll be able to share your progress, questions and successes with your study group, and provide support for others.

The courses on offer have been carefully selected to be interesting and relevant to library users, and will lead to developing useful skills that can be applied in the online and offline worlds. Learners can choose to join any of the study groups.
